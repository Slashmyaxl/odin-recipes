Macaroni & Cheese

Description

This dairy-free version of a classic comfort dish packs a punch of garlic flavor and goes well with the optional addition of mushrooms and/or broccoli. Inspired by Minimalist Baker, I've modified this into a dish I like to dub "World Famous," as a Portuguese friend of mine has made and served it to her loved ones overseas. Comforting, warming, and delicous.

Ingredients

  • 2 heads garlic, roasted
  • 10 oz. elbow macaroni noodles (8oz. if adding mushrooms/broccoli)
  • 1/4 cup oil (grapeseed, sunflower, or olive)
  • 4 cloves raw garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups almond milk (unflavored, unsweetened)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 cup cashew parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup pankp bread crumbs

Directions

  1. Roast garlic at 400 degrees F for 45 minutes, then let cool.
  2. Cook macaroni in salted boiling water per package instructions and preferences (i.e. al dente vs. soft). Drain and cover.
  3. In a large skillet over medium-low heat, add oil. Once hot, add raw garlic. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add flour, whisk into oil, and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Slowly incorporate almond milk, adding 1/2 cup at a time, then whisking. Repeat until all milk is used. Cook for 2 minutes, stir frequently.
  6. Transfer contents of skillet to blender. Add roasted garlic, salt, peper, cashew parmesan cheese, and nutritional yeast. Blend until smooth and creamy. Taste and add seasonings to desired taste.
  7. Transfer contents of blender back to skillet. Cook and stir frequently for 2-3 minutes until sauce is thick.
  8. Fold in cooked pasta (and mushrooms/broccoli if using) until coated in sauce.
  9. Remove from heat. Top with panko bread crumbs and 1 Tbsp nutritional yeast.
  10. Broil in oven for 1-2 minutes until top is golden-brown, careful not to burn.
  11. Top with smoked paprika (optional), serve, and enjoy!
