Roasted Potato Wedges

Description

A comforting appetizer for all to enjoy! One family member of mine has endearingly dubbed these "Perfect Potatoes," making me especially proud whenever I get the browning just right. All together tasty, healthy, and nutritious because I don't care what anyone says - potatoes are vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 3 russet potatoes
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Wash and dry potatoes, slice into halves three times if potatoes are large (you should end up with 8 wedges per potato). Depending on size of potatoes, you may want to slice so that each potato yields 6 wedges
  3. In large mixing bowl, add potato wedges, then olive oil. Mix/toss until all wedges are coated in oil.
  4. Add salt and pepper, then mix/toss again. Add cumin, then mix/toss until seasoning appears evenly distributed accross potato wedges.
  5. Place wedges on baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Cook at 400 degrees F for 18-22 minutes, until underside of wedges are golden-brown. Flip each wedge onto opposite side.
  7. Continue cooking on opposite sides for additional 18-22 minutes, until potato wedges are roughly golden-brown all around.
