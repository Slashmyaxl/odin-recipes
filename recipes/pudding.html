Avocado Chocolate Pudding

Description

A healthy(-ish), sweet dessert for all to enjoy. This chocolate pudding was inspired by Chocolate Covered Katie and modified to the liking of all of my friends. Simple, easy, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 240g ripe avocado flesh
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp almond extract (optional)
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup agave syrup

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients into blender, blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Taste, adding more agave syrup until desired sweetness reached.
  3. Enjoy! - this keeps best refrigerated within an airtight container.
